Description

Nasi Goreng (Indonesian Fried Rice)

Nasi Goreng is a popular Indonesian dish made from pre-cooked rice stir-fried with a blend of savory spices, sweet soy sauce (kecap manis), garlic, shallots, and chili. Often served with fried egg on top, it can include various proteins such as chicken, shrimp, beef, or tofu. Known for its smoky aroma and rich flavor, nasi goreng is commonly enjoyed as a hearty breakfast, lunch, or dinner. It's typically garnished with crispy shallots, sliced cucumber, tomatoes, and prawn crackers for added texture and taste. Beloved across Southeast Asia, nasi goreng is a comforting, flavorful meal that reflects Indonesia's vibrant culinary heritage.

Since there are so many variations of Nasi Goreng, this time I'll share a simple recipe.

Ingredients :

  • 2 portion of rice
  • 50gr shredded chicken
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cloves of shallot, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • 1 curly chili
  • 1 tablespoon of sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 2 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of soy sauce
  • 1/2-1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of margarin
  • 1 teaspoon of mushroom bouillon
  • cooking oil for sautéing

How to Make (Steps) :

  1. Step 1 :
    Heat a small amount of oil in a pan. Add the chicken and sauté until fully cooked. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  2. Step 2 :
    In the same pan, heat a little more oil. Add the eggs and scramble them until cooked through.
  3. Step 3 :
    Add the garlic, onions, and chilies into the pan. Stir and sauté until fragrant and soft.
  4. Step 4 :
    Add the rice to the pan.Stir until well combined with the other ingredients.
  5. Step 5 :
    Add the shredded chicken back into the pan. Then add sweet soy sauce, soy sauce, salt, pepper, and mushroom seasoning. Mix everything thoroughly.
  6. Step 6 :
    Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Once the flavor is balanced, remove from heat.
  7. Step 6 :
    Serve hot with fresh cucumber slices or lettuce on the side.
